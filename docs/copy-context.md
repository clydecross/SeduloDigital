## Brand Voice
Tone descriptors: Direct. Grounded. Unpretentious.
Writing style: Short declarative sentences. Fragments when they land harder. Active voice. Specific verbs. Contractions are natural. Varied sentence length for rhythm.

## Voice Rules
- Write like a real human speaking clearly and confidently
- Use contractions when appropriate (you're, it's, we're, that's)
- Vary sentence length for natural rhythm
- No em dashes anywhere — not a single one
- No buzzwords: passionate, leverage, optimize, innovative, cutting-edge, synergy, ecosystem
- Never sound salesy or exaggerated
- Lead with transformation, not features
- Use "you" language throughout
- If it sounds like AI wrote it, rewrite it

## Words to Use
Real. Built. Drives. Clear. Dialed in. Ranks. Works. Ships. Grows. Handles. Earns. Matters.

## Words to Avoid
Passionate. Leverage. Innovative. Cutting-edge. Synergy. Ecosystem. Best-in-class. Unlock. Supercharge. Seamless. Holistic. Partner (overused). Solutions (overused).
